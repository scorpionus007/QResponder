# Personnel Security & Awareness Training

**Owner:** People Operations & Security · **Review cadence:** annually.

## Hiring

- **Background checks:** all employees undergo a background check prior to or upon
  hire, to the extent permitted by local law.
- **Confidentiality:** employees and contractors sign confidentiality/NDA agreements
  and acknowledge the acceptable use and information security policies on hire.

## Security awareness

- **Onboarding training:** all new hires complete security awareness training within
  their first weeks, covering phishing, data handling, password/MFA hygiene, and
  incident reporting.
- **Annual training:** all staff complete security awareness training **at least
  annually**, and engineers complete additional secure-coding training.
- **Phishing simulations:** periodic simulated phishing campaigns are run, with
  follow-up training for those who need it.

## Access and offboarding

Access is granted on the principle of least privilege and reviewed quarterly. On
termination, access is revoked within 24 hours (SSO disabled immediately on the last
day) and company assets are returned or wiped.

## Endpoints

Employee laptops are managed and required to have full-disk encryption, screen-lock,
automatic updates, and endpoint protection. Devices that fall out of compliance are
flagged and remediated.

## Culture

Security responsibilities are part of every role. Staff are encouraged to report
concerns to security@sample.example without fear of reprisal (blameless culture).
