# Privacy & Data Processing

**Owner:** Legal & Security · **Review cadence:** annually.

## Role and scope

For customer data processed on the platform, Sample Inc. acts as a **data processor**
(or "service provider"), processing personal data on behalf of customers (the
controllers) solely to provide the service and per the customer's instructions.

## Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

Sample Inc. offers a **Data Processing Agreement** that includes the EU Standard
Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for international transfers, a subprocessor list, security
commitments, and breach-notification terms. A DPA is available to all customers on
request and is incorporated into the standard terms for Enterprise customers.

## Data subject rights

Sample Inc. supports customers in fulfilling data-subject requests (access,
correction, deletion, export). Tools are provided to export and delete personal data;
on customer request or account termination, personal data is deleted within 30 days
(see Data Retention & Disposal).

## Regulations

Sample Inc.'s program is designed to support customer compliance with **GDPR** and
**CCPA/CPRA**. Sample Inc. does not sell personal data and does not use customer
content to train third-party AI models.

## Data collected

The platform processes the business data customers choose to upload, plus account and
usage metadata needed to operate and secure the service. Data collection is minimized
to what is necessary. Sensitive categories of data are not required by the service.

## International transfers and residency

US data residency is the default; EU data residency is available on Enterprise plans.
Cross-border transfers rely on the SCCs and appropriate safeguards.
