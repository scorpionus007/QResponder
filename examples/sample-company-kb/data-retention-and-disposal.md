# Data Retention & Disposal Policy

**Owner:** Security & Legal · **Review cadence:** annually.

## Customer data

- **During the subscription:** Sample Inc. retains customer data for as long as the
  customer's account is active, or as required to provide the service.
- **After termination:** customer data is deleted within **30 days** of contract
  termination, unless a longer period is required by law. On request, Sample Inc. will
  provide an export of customer data before deletion.
- **Deletion method:** data is removed from production systems and rendered
  unrecoverable; encrypted backups containing the data are purged on the standard
  backup rotation (within 30 days).

## Operational and log data

- **Application and audit logs:** retained for **12 months**, then deleted.
- **Security monitoring data:** retained per the monitoring policy (typically 12
  months) to support investigations.
- **Backups/snapshots:** retained for 30 days on a rolling basis.

## Media disposal

Sample Inc. runs on cloud infrastructure; physical media sanitization is handled by
AWS under its certifications (data-bearing devices are decommissioned per NIST 800-88).
Any company-owned endpoints are wiped or destroyed before disposal.

## Data minimization

Sample Inc. collects only the data necessary to provide the service and does not sell
customer data. Personal data handling is described in the Privacy & Data Processing
document.
