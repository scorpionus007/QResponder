# Sample Inc. — Security Program Overview

Sample Inc. is a B2B SaaS company founded in 2018, headquartered in Austin, Texas,
with approximately 180 employees. Our platform helps mid-market companies manage
their operations, and we process customer business data on their behalf.

## Security program

Sample Inc. maintains a formal information security program aligned to the AICPA
Trust Services Criteria and ISO/IEC 27001. The program is owned by the VP of
Security (CISO) and reviewed by executive leadership quarterly. A cross-functional
Security Committee meets monthly to review risks, incidents, and policy exceptions.

## Certifications and attestations

- **SOC 2 Type II** — Security, Availability, and Confidentiality criteria; audited
  annually by an independent CPA firm. The most recent report covers a 12-month
  observation period with no exceptions noted.
- **ISO/IEC 27001** — certification in progress; target completion within the current
  fiscal year.
- Annual third-party penetration test and a continuous vulnerability management
  program.

## Hosting and data residency

The platform runs entirely on Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the `us-east-1` and
`us-west-2` regions, deployed across multiple Availability Zones. Customer data is
stored in the United States by default; EU data residency is available on Enterprise
plans.

## Where to find specifics

Detailed controls are documented in the individual policies in this knowledge base,
including encryption, access control, incident response, business continuity, data
retention, vendor risk, and secure development. Sample Inc. will provide its current
SOC 2 report and other evidence under NDA on request.
